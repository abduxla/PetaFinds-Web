FAQ

Good questions, clear answers.

Is PetaFinds free?

Yes. Downloading and browsing is completely free — you don't even need an account to explore shops and products.

Can I buy inside the app?

Not yet. PetaFinds is a discovery app — you find products and sellers, then contact or visit the shop directly. There's no cart or checkout.

How do businesses get listed?

Create a free business profile, add your products with photos and prices, and manage everything from your dashboard. Customers can then find you in search and on the map.

Is it only for Pettah?

We're starting with Pettah and Colombo's wholesale market — Sri Lanka's busiest trading hub — with more areas to follow.

Which devices is it on?

PetaFinds is live on the App Store for iPhone now. Android is on the way.
