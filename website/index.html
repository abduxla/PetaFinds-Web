FAQ

Clear answers before launch.

What is PetaFinds?

PetaFinds is a discovery and listing-first platform for Pettah businesses and wholesale products.

Is PetaFinds a shopping app?

Not yet. PetaFinds helps customers discover products and sellers; it does not include cart or checkout for now.

Can customers buy inside the app?

No cart, checkout, or payment is available for now. Customers discover products, then contact or visit the business.

How do businesses add products?

Business owners create a profile, add product details, upload images, and manage listings from their dashboard.

Is it only for Pettah?

PetaFinds starts with Pettah and Colombo's wholesale market, with local discovery as the focus.

When will the app launch?

Launch timing will be announced soon. Store links are easy to replace when the app pages are ready.
