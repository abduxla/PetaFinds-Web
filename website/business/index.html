How it works

Everything runs through your Business Portal.

No card details, no auto-billing. Pay by bank transfer, upload your receipt in the portal, and a human verifies it — usually within 24 hours.

1
Sign in to the portal

Use your PetaFinds business account at petafinds.lk/portal. Your dashboard shows your plan, renewal date and benefits.

2
Pay & upload your receipt

Bank details are right there on the Payments page. Transfer, then attach your slip with the reference number — it takes a minute.

3
We verify & you're live

Our team confirms the payment, your plan activates instantly and a numbered invoice lands in your Invoice Centre and inbox.
