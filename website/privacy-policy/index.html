Privacy Policy

PetaFinds Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 27/04/2026

PetaFinds is a mobile product discovery platform for Pettah, Colombo, Sri Lanka. This Privacy Policy explains how PetaFinds collects, uses, stores, and protects information.

1. What PetaFinds Does

PetaFinds helps users search for products listed by independent businesses. PetaFinds does not sell products, process payments, deliver goods, or act as the seller.

2. Information We Collect

Users may browse PetaFinds without creating an account.

If a user creates an account, we may collect:

  • name
  • email address
  • phone number, if provided
  • saved products or app preferences

For businesses, we may collect:

  • business name
  • owner or manager contact details
  • business address
  • phone number
  • WhatsApp number
  • product listings
  • product images
  • product descriptions and prices

We may also collect technical and usage information, including:

  • device type
  • operating system
  • app interactions
  • search terms
  • product views
  • business page views
  • call or WhatsApp button clicks
  • approximate location from IP address
  • crash and performance data

3. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • operate the app
  • show products and businesses
  • allow businesses to manage listings
  • improve search results
  • measure product views and business engagement
  • detect misuse or suspicious activity
  • respond to support requests
  • comply with legal obligations

4. Analytics and Third-Party Services

PetaFinds may use third-party services such as Firebase, Google Analytics for Firebase, Firebase Crashlytics, Firebase Cloud Storage, Firestore, and Algolia.

These services may process technical, usage, and listing data to help the app function, search products, store data, and improve reliability.

5. Business Contact Information

Business contact details may be displayed publicly inside the app so users can contact businesses directly.

6. Data Sharing

PetaFinds does not sell personal information.

We may share information only when necessary:

  • with service providers that help operate the app
  • to comply with law or legal requests
  • to investigate misuse, fraud, or security issues
  • as part of a business transfer, merger, or restructuring

7. User-Generated and Business-Generated Content

Businesses are responsible for the product listings, images, descriptions, prices, and claims they upload.

Users should verify product details directly with businesses before purchasing.

8. Data Retention

We keep information only as long as needed to operate the app, provide services, resolve disputes, enforce policies, or meet legal obligations.

9. Account Deletion

If account creation is available, users may request account deletion from inside the app or by contacting support at support@petafinds.lk.

Deleting an account may remove personal account data, but some records may be retained where required for legal, security, fraud prevention, dispute, or business record purposes.

10. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect data. However, no digital platform can guarantee complete security.

11. Children

PetaFinds is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

12. International Use

If users access PetaFinds outside Sri Lanka, their information may be processed in countries where our service providers operate.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted in the app or through another reasonable notice method.

14. Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact:

Email: support@petafinds.lk
Business Name: PetaFinds (Pvt) (Ltd)

Report and Takedown

PetaFinds Report and Takedown Process

Effective Date: 27/04/2026

1. Reporting a Product

Users may report a product if they believe it is:

  • fake
  • counterfeit
  • illegal
  • misleading
  • wrongly priced
  • using stolen images
  • listed under the wrong category

2. What Happens After a Report

PetaFinds may:

  • log the report
  • review the product
  • contact the business
  • request proof
  • temporarily hide the product
  • permanently remove the product
  • suspend the business

3. Emergency Removal

PetaFinds may immediately remove listings that appear illegal, harmful, counterfeit, deceptive, or risky to users or the platform.

4. No Guarantee

PetaFinds may decide whether action is necessary. A report does not automatically mean a listing will be removed.

5. Contact

Reports may be submitted in-app or by email: support@petafinds.lk
